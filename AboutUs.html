ERamen, with its roots dating back to 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia in 2015, proudly stands as one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. Our commitment to authenticity and excellence is showcased in every aspect of our menu. Utilizing organic flour, we transform humble ingredients into delectable ramen noodles that captivate the senses. At ERamen, customers can indulge in a wide variety of ramen styles, each crafted with meticulous care, as well as an enticing assortment of tempura dishes. Our vision is to become a global symbol of authentic ramen mastery, celebrated for our rich heritage, unparalleled quality, and unwavering dedication to culinary brilliance. With a mission to create extraordinary ramen experiences, we strive to delight our guests through a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, infusing every bowl with exceptional flavors, exquisite craftsmanship, and heartfelt hospitality. Through our relentless pursuit of ramen perfection, we aim to redefine the art of ramen and share its joy with passionate food enthusiasts worldwide.
